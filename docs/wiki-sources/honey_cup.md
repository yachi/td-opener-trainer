## Overview
Honey Cup is a TST to TSD opener. The Perfect Clear rate is 90%, and if T comes early in the third bag, one can make a T-Spin Double beforehand. In the first bag, O is needed before J and I then L.

## Second Bag
If one cannot make one of the above stacks, try one of these stacks. The first seven stacks can result in a Perfect Clear.

Alternatively, one can convert it to a Center 4-Wide with six residuals.

## Third Bag
The rate for the standard stack is 96% and if T comes as the first or second piece, chances are 100%. For most solutions, the I piece can be placed at anytime.

## After 3rd Bag PC (DPC)
After a 3rd bag Perfect Clear and an O is left unused, one can use this technique afterwards. This technique can also be used after Gamushiro Stacking, PC-Spin, Black Tea Cannon or even Triple Triple PC, to name a few examples. In the fifth bag, there are various solutions, so memorize the ones with S and Z being used together.
