## Overview
Stray Cannon (迷走砲) is a TD opener made by kuimono_hoshiii (くいものくれ). 75% build rate on bag 1 (90% with mirror) if S/Z is held. Varying routes on bag 2. **High chances of Perfect Clear on bag 3.**

## Bag 1
Keep either S or Z in hold.

## Bag 2 (PC) — 3 main routes with 100% cover, plus 2 extras

### J>O, 97.94% PC (Standard)
- 66.66% chance to stack with TST
- Remaining field same as **TST B of Olive Stacking and Setup C of Mountainous Stacking 2**
- I piece placement is fixed
- 15 minimals with per-minimal PC rates (39.6% down to 2.54%)

### S>J, 84.25% PC (Standard)
- 66.66% chance to stack with TST
- J piece placement is fixed
- 7 minimals (55.44% down to 9.52%)

### O>SJ, 70.63% PC (Standard)
- Lower PC chance — prioritize other two standard routes when possible
- 50% chance to stack with TST
- I and J piece placements both fixed
- 5 minimals (47.62% down to 5.71%)

### S>O IZ>J, 85.28% PC (Extra)
- 43.33% chance to stack with TST
- 8 minimals (47.62% down to 2.54%)

### J>I, 53.41% PC (Extra)
- 66.66% chance to stack with TST
- Remaining field same as **TST E of Olive Stacking**
- J piece placement is fixed
- 5 minimals (26.19% down to 9.52%)
