## Overview
Gamushiro Stacking (ガムシロ積み) is a T-Spin opener created by Mr. Goods. Triple Double Attack opener. In the first bag, only the J piece is required before the S. Mirror is always stackable.

## First Bag
J before S (L before Z mirrored). L piece held, used with second bag.

## Second Bag
The first 2 ways are the most desirable. The first 4 ways can result in a Perfect Clear.

## Third Bag (Perfect Clear)
In the case of the first 4 forms mentioned, you can get a Perfect Clear after 8 total lines.

| Form | Condition | PC rate | Minimals |
|------|-----------|---------|----------|
| L → O | L before O | 99.09% | 22 |
| J → O | J before O | 96.94% | 19 |
| S → O | S before O | 86.59% | 15 |

## Third Bag and later — Other continuations

### Form A1/A2 (L → O)
- T-Spin Triple
- Imperial Cross
- Center 4-wide

### Form B (J → O)
- Nil-cross
- Trinity

### Form C (S → O)
(covered under main Third Bag)

### Form D (O → L)
- **T-Spin Triple**: In the third bag, the O piece must come before Z and S before J.
- **Double Triple Cannon**: In the third bag, the J piece must come before S and Z before O. Fourth bag mostly stackable. Fails if J before T, S and O; requires a late T.
- **Double Triple Cannon 2**: Third and fourth bags stacked same way, except T comes early in fourth bag. Fourth bag always stackable given early T.

## Perfect Clear after 10 or 14 lines

### 10-line PC (63.73% rate, 22 minimals)
Continuation from Form A or C. Third bag: S before J, O before Z then I, or for Form C: O and Z before L and I.

### 14-line PC (100.00% rate, 10 minimals)
Fourth bag: only S before Z. I, J, L, O in any order. Solutions same as Riviclia's Pelican.

## Ideal shape → Always 14-lines PC
- 3rd bag: perform TSS (lower firepower, always stackable)
- 4th bag: always Tetris
- 5th bag PC: 100.00% PC rate, TetrisPC 76.67%, 2 filter solutions

## Compromise shape subspecies → Always 14-lines PC
- 2nd bag: distinctive L-piece placement enables 14-line PC
- 3rd bag: always TSS, stack O-pieces last
- 4th bag: always Tetris
- 5th bag PC: 100.00% PC rate, 5 filter solutions
